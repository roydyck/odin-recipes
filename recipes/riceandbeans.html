Rice and Beans

Description

Rice and beans is the staple food of Belize. It is both affordable and makes up a complete protein even though it is vegan; not to mention, it is very delicious.

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Red kidney beans
  • Coconut oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add coconut oil to pan and raise to temperature
  2. Add rice and cook until done
  3. Boil beans in water until cooked
  4. Add beans to rice
  5. Salt as needed
